About

Welcome to the ultimate music discovery application!

Are you tired of listening to the bands suggested by Pitchfork?
Do you want to be the first one to discover fresh sounds before anybody else?
I LISTEN TO SONGS THAT DON'T EVEN EXIST YET lets you listen to the newest and rarest songs on the Web.

Choose a genre and enjoy the list of rare tracks collected for you.
Wanna know if you're the first person to listen to the current song? Click the Info button of the player and check how many people have listened to the current track before.
